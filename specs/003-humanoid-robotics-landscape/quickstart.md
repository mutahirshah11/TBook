# Quickstart: Contributing to Chapter 3

**Goal**: Edit or verify the "Humanoid Robotics Landscape" chapter.

## Prerequisites
1.  **Repo Clone**: Ensure you have the repo cloned and dependencies installed (`npm install`).
2.  **Server**: Run `npm start` to see the Docusaurus site.

## Editing Content
-   **File**: `docs/part1/chapter3-humanoid-landscape.mdx` (To be created).
-   **Images**: Place images in `static/img/chapter3/`.
-   **Components**: Reusable components are in `src/components/`.

## Verification Steps
1.  **Check Links**: Ensure internal links to Ch 1 and Ch 2 work.
2.  **Check Tone**: Must be Grade 10-12 reading level. No jargon without definition.
3.  **Check Mobile**: Inspect the taxonomy tables on mobile view.
